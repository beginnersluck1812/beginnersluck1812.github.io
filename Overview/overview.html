What is the Goal of Our Program?

The goal of our program is to find the best move in any given position of the game were playing.

More simply we want to give our program a position and have it output the best move

How Humans Achieve This Goal

Now when we play board games we share the same goal. To look at the board and find the best move. So lets look at an example of how a humnan might play Tic-Tac-Toe

Look at this position in which your playing as X. What is the best move?


Got it.... good. Now unless you've spent countless hours playing against your siblings as a kid odds are you had to do a bit thinking to come up with a solution.

Quickly looking at the board you probably noticed that there weren't any obvious moves that you could make. 'O' wasn't threatening you and you couldn't win in one move. So what did you have to do? Think

More specifically you had to think ahead in the game and weigh the possible moves you could make with the responses 'O' could make in the future.

You picked a move in your head, calculated the responses 'O' could make, then calculated the responses you could make to the responses 'O' could make. Then you calculated the responses... well you get the idea.

By plotting out the future moves both players could make, you were able to find the best move.

How our Program is Going to Achieve Its Goal

Our program is going to the same exact thing we do. It is going to take a position and then compute all the possible future moves and responses that both players can make.
